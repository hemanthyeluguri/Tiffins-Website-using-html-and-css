Bajji

A crispy, deep-fried snack made with a variety of vegetables (such as potato, onion, or chili), coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter. Bajji is a popular street food in many parts of India and is served hot with tangy chutneys or spicy masala.

Idly

Soft, steamed rice cakes made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. Idly is a traditional South Indian breakfast, served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar. It’s light, nutritious, and an excellent source of energy.

Puri

Deep-fried, puffy bread made from wheat flour, served with a variety of sides like potato curry (Aloo Sabzi), chole (chickpeas), or chutneys. Puri is a staple in many Indian households for breakfast, and it’s filling and flavorful.
